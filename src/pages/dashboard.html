Enable Applescript on Your Browsers

To enable granular storage, you will need to allow applescript execution in Brave, Chrome and Safari.

For Brave and Chrome:

  1. Open the browsers (Brave and Chrome)
  2. Go to "View"
  3. Go to "Developer"
  4. Click on "Allow Javascript from Apple Events"
  5. Restart Chrome, Brave and Infr

For Safari:

  1. Open the Safari Browser
  2. Go to "Develop"
  3. Click on "Allow Javascript from Apple Events"
  4. Restart Safari and Infr
